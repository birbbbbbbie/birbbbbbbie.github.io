Outreach

FLL Tournaments

  We are actively planning on mentoring more FLL and FTC teams during their qualifying season to encourage the growth of their knowledge and passion for robotics. It is really important for us to cultivate an excitement towards robotics in younger children, as it will allow them to continue pursuing their passion as they mature. Having a fundamental knowledge towards mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer aided drafting (CAD), and computer science will help boost them ahead of their peers and help them gain confidence in the STEM world.
 Especially within the last year, we have prioritized bringing FIRST to the local community. Our most recent FLL tournament took place on 10/26/19 at the Walnut Teen Center. 17 FLL teams participated and 38 volunteers helped run it. The event was a success and we look forward to hosting future tournaments so that we can not only encourage others to pursue FIRST, but to also get to know members in our community and create lasting bonds.
