Our Team

Who We Are

We are a high-school robotics team from Walnut, California. We participate in an annual FIRST robotics competition every year. Our goal is to inspire and motivate our community to be a part of the STEM field.

Subgroups

Our team has two main departments: Engineering and Business, which branch out into multiple sub-groups. The Engineering department consists of Mechanical, Electrical, and Programming, while the Business department contains Public Relations, Treasurers, Sponsors and Grants, and Event Planners.
